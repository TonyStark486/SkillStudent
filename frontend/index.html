About Us

SkillTracker is a revolutionary platform designed to transform how educational institutions manage student data. Built with MongoDB NoSQL database, we offer unparalleled flexibility in tracking student skills, projects, and achievements.

Our mission is to eliminate rigid data structures and provide a dynamic, scalable solution that grows with your institution. Whether you're tracking 100 or 10,000 students, SkillTracker adapts to your needs.

Founded in 2024, we're committed to empowering educators and students with modern technology that makes skill management effortless and insightful.
